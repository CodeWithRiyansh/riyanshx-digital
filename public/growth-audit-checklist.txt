RiyanshX Digital - 10 Website Fixes To Increase Leads

1. Make the hero headline explain the exact offer.
2. Put one clear CTA above the fold.
3. Add WhatsApp or phone action for mobile visitors.
4. Show proof close to your CTA.
5. Replace generic service text with outcome-led copy.
6. Add a lead form with business type and urgency.
7. Track every form, click, and call source.
8. Improve page speed and mobile readability.
9. Add retargeting pixels before running ads.
10. Connect every lead to a follow-up workflow.
